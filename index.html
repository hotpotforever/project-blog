Vaccination diary

I had my vaccination yesterday at the stadium. I was such a blessing to have the opportunity to get vaccinated in the midst of the pandemic like this, especially when things are becoming more and more serious. I feel safe.

Coming to the vaccination place, you need to have some documents related to your personal information, and a health declaration with a confirmation that you agree to take the vaccine completely by your own will.
There will be a notification text sent to your phone through sms and you would need to show the staffs at the vaccination place that text as prove that you are on the list for vaccination.
After entering the stadium, you will get your blood pressure test and some general question about any medication you are taking or if you are pregnant or not. Once everything is done you're good to proceed to the injection area.
The injection was not too painfull to me, it will stings a bit and the area where you get the injection will throb. Bare in mind everyone will have a different experience with the side effect.

Here are what I'm feeling so far, after roughly 20 hours of vaccination:

  • Drowsiness.
  • My body feels sore.
  • The area of injection hurts but bareable.
  • I don't have a fever.

That's all I have for you. If you want more, let me know in the comment section!
